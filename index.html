Developer Conference

Thunder Plains is a web and mobile developer conference organized by the Oklahoma City Javascript User Group. The conference focuses on JavaScript and related technologies in a wide variety of different use cases and platforms which make the web all the more interesting.

Code of Conduct

Before submitting a proposal or purchasing tickets and attending the conference, please review the Thunder Plains' Code of Conduct. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding the CoC. Our conference is a safe learning environment for everyone. The CoC applies to all official and unofficial events by Thunder Plains, and any location where attendees may be congregating. We encourage you to contact us at any time with any questions, comments, or concerns. Your anonymity and privacy will always remain protected by all Thunder Plains organizers and volunteers. The safety and well-being of attendees and others is our utmost concern, so please speak up and speak out. We're here for you.
